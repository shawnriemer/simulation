All sorts of betting strategies exist, but do any of them actually work? (Hint: no) Try your luck by simulating various strategies below.

None

The "No Strategy" strategy where you roll up to a table and play until you inevitably run out of money. Most people fall in this category.

{{ graph_none|safe }}

Martingale

Double your bet after every loss to instantly recoup all your losses. I thought I was a genius in elementary school for coming up with this idea. Surprisingly it turns out I am not a genius, as this strategy just assures you lose your money all at once instead of gradually.

{{ graph_martingale|safe }}

Grand Martingale

In addition to doubling your bet after losses, add the number of units equal to how many consecutive losses you've had. This way you stay on the same path regardless of how many times you lose. It also makes prettier graphs than generic Martingale.
